THEY LINGER ON THE MIND

WOODEN SOUVENIR

This is a souvenir I acquired from a visit to China. It’s a wooden block attached to red woven threads.

The two sides of the block read “祭祖平安符” (Jì zǔ píng'ān fú) and “贾姓图腾” (Jiǎ xìng túténg). The first side means “Ancestral Peace Talisman”, while the other reads “Jia Totem”. These words represent the act of visiting a specific location to pay respect to your ancestors and your surname. This object holds importance to me as it pertains to my name (Jia) in particular, and paying respects to my family name. Without understanding the words on the talisman, the object appears to just be some decorative block. But with the understanding of its content, it reveals the aspect of life and the passage of life through generations. It lingers on the mind.
